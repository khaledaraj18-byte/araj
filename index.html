Case Studies

Qawafel · B2B Marketplace

Automating Retailer Disputes at Scale

Product Manager · Marketplace & Operations
+30%
Retailer Satisfaction
Problem

Retailers were losing trust in the platform. Dispute resolution was manual, inconsistent, and slow — hurting retention and burying the support team in repetitive escalations.

Role

Owned the marketplace roadmap end-to-end. Led discovery with retailers and support, then drove cross-functional execution with engineering, design, and operations.

Decision

Built automated dispute management and support workflows — clear SLAs, status visibility, auto-routing, and a self-serve resolution path for the most common cases.

Result

+30% retailer satisfaction, faster resolution cycles, reduced support load, and stronger marketplace retention.

Shgardi · Last-Mile Logistics

Cutting Delayed Orders by Two-Thirds

Business Analyst · Operations & Logistics
−66%
Delayed Orders
Problem

A high share of orders were being delivered late. Customer trust was eroding, and the team had no shared view of why orders missed their SLA.

Role

Owned the delayed-orders project end-to-end. Coordinated with finance, logistics, courier ops, and partner support to define the problem and the measurement model.

Decision

Built a delay-reason tracking system, segmented root causes (courier, partner, address, peak hours), then shipped targeted operational and product fixes for each segment.

Result

−66% delayed orders, measurable improvement in on-time delivery, and a sharper operational picture for the leadership team.
